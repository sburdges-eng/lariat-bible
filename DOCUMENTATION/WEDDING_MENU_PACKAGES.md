## üí° TIPS FOR CLIENTS

### Choosing Your Menu:
1. **Consider your venue**: Outdoor = lighter options, Indoor = more flexibility
2. **Know your crowd**: Adventurous eaters vs. traditional preferences
3. **Match your theme**: Rustic = BBQ, Elegant = Prime Rib, Fun = Taco Bar
4. **Season matters**: Heavy in winter, light in summer
5. **Don't forget vegetarians**: Always have a strong non-meat option

### Guest Count Guidelines:
- **Food quantity**: Our portions are generous, everyone leaves satisfied
- **Buffet lines**: 150+ guests = consider dual stations
- **Cocktail hour**: More passed apps for larger groups
- **Leftovers**: We can package extras for you

### Popular Combinations:
- **Most Popular**: Southwestern Elegance (taco bar)
- **Most Elegant**: Southern Celebration (prime rib)
- **Most Unique**: New Mexico Sunset (green chile pork)
- **Best Value**: Grand Fiesta (dual stations)
- **Summer Favorite**: Coastal Romance (seafood boil)
